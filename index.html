About Me

"Self-taught Web Developer with a passion for creating clean, elegant solutions that actually work for End Users"

After a decade of working in the Oil and Gas sector - the majority of the time in Project Management - I grew frustrated with inefficiencies in various software and web tools that were being used. I started out writing VBA macro here and there to optimize an excel sheet, or performing a tweak to one of the internal processes and business systems. Soon this grew into a passion for designing content that actually worked for end users in a clean and efficient manner and evenntually started me on a web development journey.

Over the past couple of years I have taken a number of courses in Full Stack Web Design and applied the skills that I learned across various projects - this site being one of them. My engineering background means I love to solve problems in practical ways which I believe is an important start point for any web development challenge.
